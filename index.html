Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

OneLine was built on a simple premise: your thoughts and memories are yours alone. We believe that a journal should be a private sanctuary, free from tracking, data harvesting, or cloud surveillance.

The OneLine Promise: We don't want your data. We don't see your data. We don't sell your data.

1. Data Storage

All journal entries you write in OneLine are stored locally on your device. We do not use our own servers to store your content. Your entries never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export them or if you have enabled system-level backups (like iCloud Backup) on your iPhone.

2. No Tracking & Analytics

OneLine does not include any third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app, when you open it, or what you write. We prioritize a quiet, focused experience over data collection.

3. Subscriptions (Pro)

OneLine uses Apple’s standard StoreKit to manage Pro subscriptions. Apple handles the payment processing and gives us a unique identifier to know if you are a Pro user. This data is managed by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Security

Because your data is stored locally, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including disk encryption and your passcode/FaceID.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the official support channels.
